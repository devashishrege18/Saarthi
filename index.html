Core Capabilities

Built for accounts payable.
Engineered for trust.

Inbox-First Architecture

Connect your corporate Gmail or Outlook once. SAARTHI monitors your inbox continuously, detecting financial documents the moment they arrive — no file uploads, no manual routing.

OAuth 2.0 · Zero-touch

Document Intelligence

Multi-modal extraction handles digital PDFs, scanned images, and spreadsheets. AI extracts vendor name, invoice number, amounts, tax, line items — with per-field confidence scoring.

OCR · LLM extraction

Decision DNA™

Every approval, rejection, or escalation is backed by an immutable, explainable evidence chain. No black boxes. Every decision can be audited, questioned, and justified.

Explainable AI · Audit trail

Trust Score Engine

A composite trust index — weighted across extraction confidence, validation rules, vendor history, and fraud signals — determines autonomous approval or human escalation.

Deterministic · Weighted scoring

Human-in-the-Loop Review

When trust falls below threshold, SAARTHI escalates to a human reviewer — presenting the evidence, flagged anomalies, and Decision DNA™ to make the review fast and informed.

Selective escalation · Context-rich

ERP-Ready Output

Approved invoices are structured for direct ERP ingestion — vendor name, line items, tax, PO number, and payment terms exported in a standard format ready for SAP, Oracle, or Tally.

SAP · Oracle · Tally ready

Design Philosophy

AI only where
reasoning is required.

SAARTHI uses AI selectively — for semantic extraction and pattern detection. Every business rule is deterministic, auditable, and explainable. No probabilistic black boxes in the decision path.

"AI is used only where semantic reasoning is required. Business logic is deterministic and explainable."
01

Explainability First

Every decision has a traceable, auditable evidence chain. No decision is ever "just the model said so."

02

Deterministic Core

Validation, scoring, and routing are pure logic. AI handles extraction. Rules handle decisions.

03

Human Authority

SAARTHI requests human review — never demands blind trust. The human always has final authority.
